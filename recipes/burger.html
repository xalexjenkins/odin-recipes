Burger Recipe!

Description

This is a very quick delicious burger recipe for anyone who wants to make some delicious patties at home for their family. Minimal ingredients and short cook time

ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 roma tomato
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Buns of course!
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Mix up the ground beef into a bowl.
  2. Mix the egg into the ground beef; this helps it keep its shape and not break.
  3. Form the patties into circular shapes. Be sure to keep them flat for an even cook. You can also put a thumb print in the center to help keep juices in
  4. Salt and pepper 1 side of the patty before putting it on the grill
  5. Put the patties on the grill; 4 minutes per side.
  6. Cut up the roma tomato into thin slices, not thicker than your patties.
  7. Break off some pieces of lettuce for some crunch
  8. Once you flip the burgers apply salt and pepper to the other side of the burger
  9. Toast some buns either on the grill or with a toaster
  10. Once the burgers are done let them sit for a minute to soak up juices again
  11. Assemble your burger and enjoy!

Other tips!

Some other stuff you can do is spice it up with some other ingredients. I Like onions and pickles but my wife doesnt. You can also get some cheese and put it on about 2 minutes before the burger is done and let it melt to one side! Enjoy!
