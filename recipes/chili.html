Chili Recipe!

Description

A delcious chili recipe that will make the whole family happy and is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef (80+)
  • 1 large onion (1 cup)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of chili beans in sauce
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and onion in skillet for 8-10 minutes or until brown, stirring occasionaly, drain.
  2. Drain mixture into slowcooker and mix in rest of Ingredients
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6 - 8 hours
  4. Enjoy!

Other tips!

You dont have to use beef! Feel free to use any other ground meat, a friend of mine likes to use venison and it is delcious!
