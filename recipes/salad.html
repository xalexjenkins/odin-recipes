Salad Recipe!

Description

A very quick salad option that tastes delicious and easy for a snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of caesar dressing
  • 2 bunches of romaine torn up into bite size pieces
  • 2 cups of garlic or caesar crutons
  • 2/3 cup of freshly grated parmesan
  • Freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Pour the dressing and lettuce into a bowl and mix until all lettuce covered
  2. Sprinkle the croutons pepper and cheese on and toss
  3. Enjoy!

Other tips!

You can add some meat in here for some extra flavor, some grilled chicken or bacon strips are always a good choice!
